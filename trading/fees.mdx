<Note>
Taker Order Fees is 0.025% (2.5bps) and Maker Order Fees is 0.05% (5bps) for all spot markets.
</Note>

A <b>Maker Order</b> is a limit order that adds liquidity to the order book by not immediately matching with an existing order, instead waiting for a counterparty to fill it.

A <b>Taker Order</b> is a market or limit order that removes liquidity from the order book by immediately matching with an existing order.
